AP 2D Art and Design Portfolio

Explanation

This is a collection of javascript projects I submitted for my ap 2d art class. The main goal of my art here was a sustained investigation into memories and the perception of time, mostly focusing on the past and future. I tried conveying time and its associated feelings with movement and warping of otherwise traditional objects. In total, I made 8 projects (the remaining 4 will be added here soon), building upon each one and increasing in complexity each time.

Piece 1: Uncertainty

This is a work in progress of a landscape based off a piece I made some time ago. There is a scrolling edition as well with a parallax effect. The image is proceduraly generated, so you can get a new picture each time you click on the canvas. Occasionally, a moon, secondary planet, and stars will appear in addition to the three spike types visible.

Piece 2: Juxtaposition

In making this, I wanted to improve a few areas from my first project. I wanted to increase overall movement, engage in different forms of interactivity, and take real-world images and incorporate them into what I make.


Piece 3: Forgetfulness

This piece focuses on the process of forgetting a memory. Press the button at the bottom of the screen to "re-remember" the memory, thereby redrawing it. This project uses a significant amount of memory, so I have it in a link instead of in the main page.
