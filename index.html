Project 1: The Vault

This was an introductory attempt to make a replayable game. Use the WASD or arrow keys to move, and use the mouse to aim and attack. There are roughly 20 levels, but you can continue past this point. After you lose, you can try again. Go through all rooms and press ENTER to descend to the next level.
